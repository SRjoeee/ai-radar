# AI News Radar — Product Context

## Register
Product — design serves the product. This is a utility dashboard, not a brand page.

## Users
AI practitioners, developers, and researchers who need a high-signal daily feed of video/audio/music/agent AI updates. They open it once a day, scan for 2-3 minutes, and close it.

## Product Purpose
A 24-hour AI signal radar that filters noise and surfaces what matters. Not another aggregator — a curated, auto-updating radar with visible source health.

## Tone
Technical, calm, efficient. No marketing language. Information density over decoration.

## Anti-References
- Generic SaaS dashboards with purple-blue gradients
- Card-heavy layouts with nested cards
- Newsletter-style email templates
- Social media feeds with engagement metrics

## Strategic Principles
- Signal over noise: fewer items, better filtered
- Source transparency: show where info comes from and whether sources are healthy
- Zero-config default: works without login, API keys, or setup
- Forkable: easy for others to customize with their own sources
